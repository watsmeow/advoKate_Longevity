Individual Coaching

The Method Perspective is person – centered: you are the main event. All the details of your life to this point matter, and we are here to listen to your story and create solutions for your specific needs and desires. This could be losing 50 pounds, lowering blood pressure, wanting to crawl around with grandkids, looking to hire the right care giver, or navigating the world of senior housing. Our coaches are trained across gerontology with multiple areas of expertise to help advoKate for you in this moment of life.

Below are the primary areas in which we focus with our individuals clients. We know, however, that life isn't seperable into distinct domains, and we truly work to make our coaching holisic in its approach.

  • Fitness and Strength Training

    Few things are as important as physical and mental strength as one ages. Contact us today for options regarding personalized or group strength training education modules, programming, or one on one training sessions. Whatever level or equipment you may have, we will create a viable solution for you.

  • Nutrition

    Nutrition is a minefield, and nourishment will look different for everyone. Allow us to create a delicious blueprint, plan, and shopping resources that are customized to your Method Perspecive.

  • Transformation & Renewment

    No matter what age you are, where you are with your career; if you are like our other clients you are looking to provide meaningful longevity to transforming yourself, your career, and/or recognize that retirement as a new journey. We will help you to find others with similar aspirations and help you to plan what this next dream chapter might entail. Reach out to start the conversation – we love making connections and empowering others to start fresh with support with a like-minded community.
